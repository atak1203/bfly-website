Talon

Our first fixed-wing platform. A ready-made airframe pushed to its limits in the Savaşan competition.

Photo Coming Soon

Overview

Talon was BFL'Y's first step into fixed-wing aviation. Built on a ready-made airframe, the team focused on integrating avionics, autonomous flight systems, and payload capabilities rather than manufacturing the structure from scratch.

Talon was our competition debut — entered into the Savaşan competition, it was the first time BFL'Y flew in a real contest environment against other teams.

Competition

Savaşan pushed the team hard. Flying a fixed-wing aircraft in a competitive setting demanded precision, reliability, and quick ground-crew decision-making. Talon delivered the experience that no classroom could — real stakes, real failures, real victories.

Transition to X

Talon's biggest contribution wasn't its performance — it was the knowledge it handed to the next generation. The lessons from integrating systems on a ready-made platform directly shaped the decision to build X entirely from scratch, with carbon fiber, from the ground up.
