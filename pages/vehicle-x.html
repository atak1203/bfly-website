X

Our most advanced aircraft. Hand-built from carbon fiber, designed for SUAS 2025 and Savaşan — fully autonomous, built entirely in-house.

Photo Coming Soon

Overview

X is BFL'Y's most ambitious project to date. Unlike Talon, every part of X was designed and manufactured by the team — from the carbon fiber airframe laid up by hand to the avionics stack integrated in-house. Nothing was bought ready-made.

X is a dual-competition aircraft, built to perform in both AUVSI SUAS 2025 and the Savaşan competition. Two very different mission profiles, one airframe — a design challenge that pushed the team to think harder about every tradeoff.

Airframe

The fuselage and wings are hand-laminated carbon fiber composite, giving X an exceptional strength-to-weight ratio. The manufacturing process was entirely student-led — from mold design to final layup and cure.

Autonomous Systems

X carries a full autonomous mission stack: waypoint navigation, obstacle avoidance, target detection and classification, and autonomous payload delivery. The software and hardware integration was developed entirely by the team.

Competition

SUAS 2025 is one of the world's most prestigious student UAV competitions, typically dominated by university teams. BFL'Y enters as a high school team — a fact the team wears as a badge, not an excuse.
