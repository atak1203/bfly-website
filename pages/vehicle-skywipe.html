2024 First Build Drone

Skywipe

Our very first aircraft. A cam-wiping drone that started it all — the project that proved BFL'Y could build.

Photo Coming Soon

Overview

Skywipe was BFL'Y's first ever aircraft project, built in 2024. Designed as a cam-wiping drone, it was never about winning a competition — it was about learning whether a group of high school students could actually build something that flies.

The answer was yes. Skywipe gave the team its first real experience with airframe construction, electronics integration, and flight testing. Every mistake made here made Talon and X better.

What We Learned

Building Skywipe taught the team the fundamentals: how to work with composite materials, how to wire a flight controller, how to iterate quickly when something breaks. It was our proof of concept — not for the judges, but for ourselves.

Legacy

Skywipe never competed, but it earned its place in the hangar. Every team member who worked on it carries the lessons forward. It's the reason X exists.
